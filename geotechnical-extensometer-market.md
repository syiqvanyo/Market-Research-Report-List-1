<p><strong>The Geotechnical Extensometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ROD EXTENSOMETERS</li><li>PROBE EXTENSOMETERS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The geotechnical extensometer market includes various types, notably rod and probe extensometers. Rod extensometers are used for measuring vertical or horizontal ground movement, featuring detachable rods that connect to a reference point. They offer high accuracy and are ideal for monitoring large-scale structures. Probe extensometers, on the other hand, are designed for subsurface applications, allowing for the assessment of soil or rock deformation at various depths. Both types are essential for understanding ground stability and environmental changes in construction and mining projects.</p></p>

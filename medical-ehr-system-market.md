<p><p>The Medical Electronic Health Record (EHR) system market can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based EHR systems are hosted on remote servers, allowing easy access from multiple locations, facilitating seamless data sharing and reducing IT maintenance costs. Conversely, on-premise EHR systems are installed locally within healthcare facilities, offering greater control over data security and customization but often requiring higher initial investment and ongoing maintenance. Both options cater to different needs and preferences of healthcare providers.</p></p>

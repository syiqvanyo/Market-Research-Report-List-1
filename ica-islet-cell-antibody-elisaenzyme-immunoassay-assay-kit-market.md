<p><p>The ICA ELISA assay market primarily consists of competitive and sandwich ELISA types. Competitive ELISA involves antigen competition for a fixed number of antibodies, offering sensitivity for low-abundance islet cell antibodies. Sandwich ELISA, on the other hand, captures the target antibodies between two layers of antibodies, providing increased specificity and robustness. The competition within the market is influenced by factors like sensitivity, specificity, ease of use, and cost-effectiveness, catering to various diagnostic and research applications in diabetes and autoimmune diseases.</p></p>

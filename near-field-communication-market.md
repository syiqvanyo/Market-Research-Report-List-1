<p><strong>The Near Field Communication Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Card Emulation</li><li>Reader Emulation</li><li>Peer-to-peer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Near Field Communication (NFC) market comprises three main types: Card Emulation, Reader Emulation, and Peer-to-Peer. Card Emulation allows devices to function like smart cards, enabling payments and access control. Reader Emulation turns NFC devices into readers, facilitating interaction with NFC tags and cards for data exchange. Peer-to-Peer enables two NFC-equipped devices to share information directly, such as transferring files or contact details. Together, these types enhance convenience and security in various applications, spanning payments to data sharing.</p></p>

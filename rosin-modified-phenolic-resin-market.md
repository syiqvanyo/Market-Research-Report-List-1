The Dissolving Type is formulated for applications requiring high solubility in organic solvents, making it suitable for coatings and adhesives. In contrast, the Cooking Type involves a process where rosin is reacted with phenolic compounds, enhancing thermal stability and mechanical properties, ideal for industrial applications like laminates and molded products. Both types cater to diverse industries, influencing demand and market dynamics.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Handheld Trace Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mass Spectroscopy</li><li>Ion Mobility Spectrometry</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld trace detector market is primarily categorized into three types: Mass Spectroscopy, Ion Mobility Spectrometry, and Others. Mass Spectroscopy identifies substances based on their mass-to-charge ratios, offering high sensitivity and specificity for trace detection. Ion Mobility Spectrometry measures the movement of ions through a gas, providing rapid analysis and real-time results. The "Others" category encompasses various emerging technologies, such as optical and thermal methods, serving specialized applications in security, defense, and environmental monitoring. Each type ensures effective trace detection for diverse industries.</p></p>

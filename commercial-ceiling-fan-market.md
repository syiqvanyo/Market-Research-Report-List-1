<p><p>The commercial ceiling fan market primarily comprises AC and DC ceiling fans. AC ceiling fans operate on alternating current and are typically more powerful, making them ideal for larger spaces. They offer robust airflow but may consume more energy. In contrast, DC ceiling fans use direct current, making them more energy-efficient and quieter during operation. They often come with advanced features like remote control and variable speed settings, appealing for modern commercial settings focused on energy savings and improved comfort.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Aspherical Microlens Array</li><li>Spherical Microlens Array</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Micro Lens Arrays market is categorized into two main types: aspherical microlens arrays and spherical microlens arrays. Aspherical microlens arrays feature non-spherical shapes that improve optical performance by reducing optical aberrations, making them suitable for high-precision applications such as imaging systems and fiber optics. In contrast, spherical microlens arrays have a uniform curvature, offering simpler design and manufacturing processes, and are commonly used in lighting and display technologies. Each type serves distinct applications based on optical requirements.</p></p>

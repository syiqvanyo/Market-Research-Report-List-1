<p><p>Surgeon cooling vests come in various types to combat heat and enhance comfort during procedures. Circulatory powered cooling vests utilize pumped fluids for continuous cooling, while cold pack cooling vests incorporate ice or gel packs for localized cooling. Phase change cooling vests use materials that absorb or release heat at specific temperatures, maintaining a consistent cooling effect. Other types may include evaporative or battery-operated vests, each designed to regulate body temperature effectively, ensuring optimal performance and comfort for surgeons.</p></p>

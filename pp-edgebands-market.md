<p><p>The PP Edgebands market is categorized into three distinct types based on thickness: thin, medium, and thick. Thin edgebands, typically under 1mm, offer flexibility and are ideal for curved surfaces, making them suitable for modern furniture. Medium edgebands, ranging from 1mm to 2mm, provide a balance of durability and aesthetics, commonly used in residential and commercial applications. Thick edgebands, exceeding 2mm, deliver enhanced sturdiness and a premium look, often utilized in high-end cabinetry and furniture designs.</p></p>

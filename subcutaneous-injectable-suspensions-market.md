<p><p>The Subcutaneous Injectable Suspensions market is primarily divided into two types: Single-Dose Injections and Multiple-Dose Injections. Single-Dose Injections are prefilled syringes or vials containing a single medication dosage, ensuring convenience and reducing contamination risks. In contrast, Multiple-Dose Injections allow for several doses to be withdrawn from a single vial, offering cost-effectiveness and convenience for treatments requiring repeated administration. Both types cater to varying patient needs and are essential in therapeutic areas such as diabetes, autoimmune disorders, and vaccinations.</p></p>

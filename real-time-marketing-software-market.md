<p><p>Real-time marketing software can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, ease of updates, and lower initial costs. They facilitate real-time data analysis and marketing execution from anywhere. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but often requiring higher upfront investment and maintenance efforts. Both types cater to different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>

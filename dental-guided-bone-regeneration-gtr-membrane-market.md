<p><p>Dental Guided Bone Regeneration (GBR) membranes are classified into various types based on their material composition and properties. Absorbable membranes gradually dissolve in the body, eliminating the need for removal. Non-absorbable membranes require surgical removal after healing. Collagen membranes, derived from animal sources, promote healing and tissue integration. Metal membranes provide strong support but may be less biocompatible. Synthetic membranes are made from polymers, offering customizable options. Periosteal allografts utilize human tissue to aid in bone regeneration.</p></p>

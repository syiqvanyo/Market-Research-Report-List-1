<p><p>The Mandelic Acid market is categorized into three types based on its optical isomers: DL Type, L Type, and D Type. DL Type includes a racemic mixture of both enantiomers, offering broad utility in various applications. L Type consists solely of the L-isomer, often preferred for its specific therapeutic benefits. D Type features only the D-isomer, primarily utilized in niche markets. These distinctions cater to diverse consumer needs, particularly in skincare, pharmaceuticals, and chemical industries.</p></p>

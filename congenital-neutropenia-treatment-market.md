<p><p>Congenital Neutropenia is a rare blood disorder characterized by a decreased number of neutrophils, which are crucial for fighting infections. The treatment landscape includes granulocyte colony-stimulating factors (G-CSFs), such as filgrastim and pegfilgrastim, which stimulate the bone marrow to produce more neutrophils. Hematopoietic stem cell transplantation is also considered for severe cases. Additionally, supportive care, such as prophylactic antibiotics and careful monitoring, is vital to manage infections.</p><p>The Congenital Neutropenia Treatment Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.8% during the forecast period.

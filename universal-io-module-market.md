<p><p>The Universal I/O Module Market encompasses two main types: digital and analog. Digital modules manage discrete signals, processing binary inputs and outputs, suitable for applications like automation and control systems. Conversely, analog modules handle continuous signals, measuring variables such as temperature and pressure, thus enabling precise monitoring and control. Both types enhance system flexibility, allowing seamless integration with various devices and sensors, which is critical for modern industrial applications and ensuring optimal performance in diverse operational environments.</p></p>

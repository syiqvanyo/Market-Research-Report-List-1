<p><p>The SWIR imaging cameras market includes two primary types: SWIR Area Scan Cameras and SWIR Line Scan Cameras. SWIR Area Scan Cameras capture entire images in one exposure, making them ideal for applications requiring detailed photographs of stationary objects. In contrast, SWIR Line Scan Cameras capture images line by line, which is suitable for high-speed applications, such as conveyor systems. Both types utilize short-wavelength infrared light for enhanced imaging capabilities in various industries like security, manufacturing, and medical applications.</p></p>

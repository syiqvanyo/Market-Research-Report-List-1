<p><p>NDT and inspection markets encompass various methods for evaluating material integrity without causing damage. Ultrasonic Testing uses sound waves to detect flaws; Visual Testing relies on direct observation for surface defects; Magnetic Particle Testing identifies surface and near-surface discontinuities using magnetic fields; Liquid Penetrant Testing reveals surface-breaking defects through dye penetration; Eddy-Current Testing assesses conductive materials using electromagnetic induction; Radiographic Testing utilizes X-rays to identify internal flaws; Acoustic Emission Testing monitors high-frequency sounds from material stress. Each method serves specific inspection needs further classified under "Other market" techniques.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Coated Abrasive Disc Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Abrasive</li><li>Super Abrasive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coated abrasive disc market comprises two main types: conventional abrasives and super abrasives. Conventional abrasives, such as aluminum oxide and silicon carbide, are widely used for general grinding and finishing tasks due to their cost-effectiveness and versatility. In contrast, super abrasives, including cubic boron nitride (CBN) and diamond, are employed for high-performance applications, offering superior efficiency and durability in machining hard materials. Both types cater to diverse industrial requirements, driving innovation and product development in the market.</p></p>

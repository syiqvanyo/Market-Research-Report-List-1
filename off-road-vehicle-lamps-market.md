<p><p>The Off-Road Vehicle Lamps market comprises three primary types: Halogen, HID (High-Intensity Discharge), and LED (Light Emitting Diode). Halogen lamps are traditional, offering warmer light but lower efficiency. HID lamps generate intense brightness with a longer lifespan and reduced energy consumption, making them popular for rugged terrains. LED lamps are increasingly favored for their durability, energy efficiency, and versatility, providing bright illumination with less heat generation. Each type caters to specific off-road needs, enhancing visibility and safety.</p></p>

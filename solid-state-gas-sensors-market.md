<p><strong>The Solid State Gas Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Galvanic Oxygen Sensor</li><li>Catalytic Bead Sensor</li><li>Semiconducting Oxide Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solid-state gas sensor market encompasses various types, each serving distinct applications. Galvanic oxygen sensors utilize electrochemical reactions to measure oxygen levels, primarily in medical and industrial settings. Catalytic bead sensors detect flammable gases through thermal conductivity changes across beads coated with catalyst materials. Semiconducting oxide sensors operate by measuring changes in electrical resistance caused by target gases interacting with semiconductor materials, making them suitable for detecting a range of gases, including volatile organic compounds.</p></p>

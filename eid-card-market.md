<p><p>The eID card market is categorized into three main types: Radio Frequency Cards (RFID), Integrated Circuit (IC) Cards, and other specialized cards. RFID cards use radio waves for communication, allowing for contactless identification and authentication, making them convenient for various applications. IC cards, on the other hand, contain embedded microchips that store data and perform secure transactions, offering higher security. Other market types may include biometric cards and smart cards, each tailored for specific identity verification and access control needs, enhancing user convenience and security.</p></p>

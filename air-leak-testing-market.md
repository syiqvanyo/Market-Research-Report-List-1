<p><p>The air leak testing market includes several key methods: </p><p>1. **Pressure Decay Testing** involves monitoring pressure changes in a sealed system to detect leaks.</p><p>2. **Helium Sniff Testing** uses helium as a tracer gas, with sensitive detectors identifying leaks by sniffing for helium escaping from a system.</p><p>3. **Nitrogen Purge Testing** employs nitrogen to pressurize a system, observing pressure drops to locate leaks. </p><p>Additionally, the market also explores other testing methods tailored for specific applications and requirements in leak detection.</p></p>

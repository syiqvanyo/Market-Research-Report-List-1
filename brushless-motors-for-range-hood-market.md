<p><p>Brushless motors for range hoods are categorized into two main types: external and built-in brushless DC motors. External brushless DC motors are located outside the range hood, offering easier maintenance and potentially quieter operation due to their distance from cooking noise. Conversely, built-in brushless DC motors are integrated directly within the range hood, providing compact design benefits and efficient airflow. Both types enhance energy efficiency, performance, and longevity, making them ideal choices for modern kitchen ventilation systems.</p></p>

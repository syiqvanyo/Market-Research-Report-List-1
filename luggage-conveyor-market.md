<p><p>The luggage conveyor market is categorized into three main types: Belt, Roller, and Other systems. Belt conveyors utilize a continuous loop of material to transport luggage, providing a smooth and efficient flow. Roller conveyors employ cylindrical rollers to facilitate movement, suitable for varying luggage sizes and weights. Other types may include modular systems or specialized designs tailored to specific airport or transport needs. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of efficiency, maintenance, and adaptability in handling luggage.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Torque Rheometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Screw Rheometer</li><li>Twin Screw Rheometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The torque rheometer market comprises two main types: single screw rheometers and twin screw rheometers. Single screw rheometers are designed for assessing viscosity and flow properties of materials using a single screw mechanism, suited for low to moderate shearing applications. In contrast, twin screw rheometers utilize two intermeshing screws to provide enhanced mixing and shear, making them ideal for complex formulations and higher viscosity materials. Both types are essential for quality control and material characterization in various industries, including plastics and polymers.</p></p>

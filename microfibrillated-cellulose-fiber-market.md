<p><p>The Microfibrillated Cellulose (MFC) Fiber Market includes two main types: Barrier Films and Performance Enhancers. Barrier Films utilize MFC to create lightweight, biodegradable materials that provide effective barriers against gases and moisture, making them ideal for food packaging and other protective applications. Performance Enhancers, on the other hand, leverage MFC's unique structural properties to improve the mechanical strength, viscosity, and stability of various products, including coatings, composites, and personal care items, enhancing their overall performance and sustainability.</p></p>

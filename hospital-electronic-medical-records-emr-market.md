<p><strong>The Hospital Electronic Medical Records (EMR) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-Premises</li><li>Cloud-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hospital Electronic Medical Records (EMR) market is divided into two primary types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises EMR systems are installed locally on hospital servers, offering full control over data and customization, but requiring significant IT resources and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based EMR systems are hosted in the cloud, providing accessibility from various devices, lower upfront costs, and reduced IT burden, while facilitating real-time updates and collaboration among healthcare providers.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>On Grid</li><li>Off Grid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The telecom power system market is divided into two main types: on-grid and off-grid systems. On-grid power systems are connected to the main electrical grid, allowing telecom operators to utilize grid electricity while reducing energy costs and ensuring reliability. Off-grid systems, on the other hand, are independent and often powered by renewable sources like solar or wind, providing energy access in remote areas where grid connectivity is lacking. Both types are crucial for ensuring uninterrupted telecom services.</p></p>

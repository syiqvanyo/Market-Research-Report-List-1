<p><strong>The Shelf Life Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Real Time</li><li>Accelerated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shelf life testing encompasses two primary market types: real-time and accelerated testing. Real-time testing involves observing products under normal storage conditions to determine their longevity, providing precise data on shelf life as it reflects actual usage. In contrast, accelerated testing simulates aging by exposing products to elevated temperatures and humidity levels, enabling quicker assessments of deterioration. Both methods are essential for ensuring product safety, quality, and compliance with regulations, catering to varying needs within the food, pharmaceutical, and cosmetic industries.</p></p>

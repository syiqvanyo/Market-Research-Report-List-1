<p><p>The resin pigments market comprises two main types: liquid pigments and powder pigments. Liquid pigments are dispersed in a liquid medium, offering ease of application and excellent color consistency, making them suitable for industries like coatings and inks. Powder pigments, on the other hand, are solid particles that require mixing with a binding agent, providing versatility and durability in various applications, including plastics and ceramics. Both types are crucial for enhancing aesthetics and performance across a wide range of products.</p></p>

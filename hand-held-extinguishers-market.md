<p><p>The hand-held extinguishers market includes three primary types: Dry Chemical, Foam, and Carbon Dioxide. Dry Chemical extinguishers are versatile and effective against various fires, making them popular in residential and commercial settings. Foam extinguishers are ideal for flammable liquid fires, forming a barrier that suppresses flames. Carbon Dioxide extinguishers are effective for electrical and flammable liquid fires, displacing oxygen to suffocate the flames without leaving residue. Each type is designed to address specific fire hazards, ensuring safety in diverse environments.</p></p>

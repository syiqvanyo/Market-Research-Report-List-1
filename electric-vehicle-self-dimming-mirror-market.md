<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Self-dimming Mirror market comprises two primary types: outer self-dimming mirrors and inside self-dimming mirrors. Inside self-dimming mirrors, located within the cabin, serve a similar purpose, adjusting to ambient light changes to minimize glare from headlights and improve driver visibility, contributing to overall comfort and safety during driving.</p></p>

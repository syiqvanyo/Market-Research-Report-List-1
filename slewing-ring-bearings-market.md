<p><p>The slewing ring bearings market can be categorized into three main types: internal gear, external gear, and others. Internal gear slewing rings feature gears positioned inside the ring, providing a compact design and enhanced load capacity, suitable for applications with limited space. External gear slewing rings have gears on the outer circumference, facilitating greater force transfer and compatibility with various machinery. The "others" category includes specialized bearings designed for unique applications, offering custom features to meet specific industry requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>Oil immersed transformers and dry type transformers are critical components in electrical distribution systems. Oil immersed transformers use mineral oil for insulation and cooling, providing effective thermal management and enabling high voltage applications. They are commonly used in substations and large industrial facilities. Dry type transformers, on the other hand, utilize air as a cooling medium and are designed for lower voltage applications, making them suitable for indoor installations and areas prone to environmental hazards. </p><p>The oil immersed transformer and dry type transformer market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for efficient power solutions and the expansion of renewable energy projects. The shift towards modernizing electrical infrastructure, as well as heightened safety standards, is also fostering demand for these transformers.

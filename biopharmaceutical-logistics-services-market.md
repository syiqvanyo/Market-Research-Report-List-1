<p><p>Biopharmaceutical logistics services encompass two primary market types: cold chain logistics and non-cold chain logistics. Cold chain logistics involves the transportation and storage of temperature-sensitive biopharmaceutical products, such as vaccines and biologics, that require strict temperature control to maintain efficacy. In contrast, non-cold chain logistics pertains to the handling of products that do not necessitate temperature regulation, typically involving stable pharmaceuticals. Both types are essential for ensuring the integrity, quality, and timely delivery of biopharmaceuticals across the supply chain.</p></p>

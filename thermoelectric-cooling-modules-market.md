<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Multi Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermoelectric cooling modules are categorized into two main types: single stage and multi-stage. Single-stage modules utilize one thermoelectric element to achieve moderate cooling capacities, making them suitable for applications like small refrigerators and electronic device cooling. In contrast, multi-stage modules use multiple thermoelectric elements stacked together to achieve lower temperatures and higher cooling performance. These are ideal for specialized applications such as laser cooling and high-performance electronics, where efficient temperature management is crucial.</p></p>

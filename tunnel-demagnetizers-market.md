<p><strong>The Tunnel Demagnetizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent-Magnet Demagnetizer</li><li>Stored-Energy Demagnetizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tunnel demagnetizers market comprises primarily two types: Permanent-Magnet Demagnetizers and Stored-Energy Demagnetizers. Permanent-Magnet Demagnetizers utilize a magnetic field generated by permanent magnets to remove magnetism from ferromagnetic materials as they pass through a tunnel-like structure. In contrast, Stored-Energy Demagnetizers employ energy stored in capacitors, releasing it briefly to create a strong, fluctuating magnetic field that demagnetizes objects efficiently. Both types are essential in various industrial applications for ensuring precise magnetization levels in components.</p></p>

<p><p>The multi-function infrared thermometer market encompasses two primary types: multi-function ear infrared thermometers and multi-function forehead infrared thermometers. Ear infrared thermometers utilize infrared technology to measure temperature via the ear canal, providing quick and accurate readings, especially in children. In contrast, forehead infrared thermometers measure temperature from the skin’s surface, allowing for non-invasive assessments from a distance. Both types are highly sought after for their ease of use, speed, and versatility in various settings, including homes and healthcare facilities.</p></p>

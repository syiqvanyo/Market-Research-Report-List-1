<p><p>Intelligent heat detectors are essential in fire safety, and they primarily fall into two market types: fixed temperature detectors and differential temperature detectors. Fixed temperature detectors activate when heat reaches a predetermined threshold, providing a straightforward response to fire conditions. In contrast, differential temperature detectors monitor changes in temperature over time, triggering alarms when rapid temperature increases are detected. Both types enhance safety by offering responsive solutions tailored to various environments, helping prevent fire-related risks effectively.</p></p>

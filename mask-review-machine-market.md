<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mask Review Machine market encompasses two primary methods: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB). The DD method involves direct comparison of masks against the physical die, ensuring precise defect identification during semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, the DB method utilizes a database of previously analyzed masks for comparison, enabling rapid assessments against established benchmarks. Both methods enhance quality control, streamline production processes, and minimize errors in semiconductor fabrication, ultimately contributing to improved yield and device reliability.</p></p>

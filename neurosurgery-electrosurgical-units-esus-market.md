<p><p>Neurosurgery Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) are essential tools for surgical procedures, utilizing high-frequency electrical currents for tissue cutting and coagulation. The market includes three main types: Monopole Electrosurgical Units, which use a single electrode for cutting and coagulating tissue; Bipolar Electrosurgical Units, which utilize two electrodes for precise energy delivery, minimizing thermal spread; and Vessel Sealing Units, designed specifically for sealing blood vessels and tissue efficiently, ensuring reduced bleeding and enhanced surgical precision. Each type plays a critical role in neurosurgical operations.</p></p>

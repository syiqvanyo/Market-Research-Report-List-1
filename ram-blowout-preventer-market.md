<p><p>The Ram Blowout Preventer (BOP) market is categorized into two main types: Flanged Ram Blowout Preventers and Studded Ram Blowout Preventers. Flanged Ram BOPs feature flanged connections that allow for secure attachment to various pipe diameters while providing easy maintenance and installation. Conversely, Studded Ram BOPs utilize threaded studs for coupling, offering flexibility in pressure handling and enhanced sealing capabilities. Both types are essential in oil and gas drilling operations for ensuring well control and preventing blowouts.</p></p>

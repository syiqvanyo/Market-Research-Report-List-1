<p><p>DNA sequencing involves determining the precise order of nucleotides in a DNA molecule. The market features various amplification methods, including emulsion PCR, bridge amplification, and single-molecule technologies. Emulsion PCR allows simultaneous amplification of numerous DNA fragments by encapsulating them in microdroplets, enhancing efficiency. Bridge amplification, used in many sequencing platforms, creates dense clusters of DNA on a solid surface. Single-molecule sequencing, meanwhile, directly reads DNA strands without amplification, enabling rapid and cost-effective analysis, catering to diverse genomic research needs.</p></p>
